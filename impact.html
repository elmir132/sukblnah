Take Care Together


In a time of unprecedented challenges, KNU students, faculty, and staff remain deeply committed to the communities the University calls home. Whether it be through cutting-edge research, support for healthcare workers, or resources for neighbors, local businesses and nonprofits, KNU is grateful to work alongside state, local, and community partners in Cambridge, Boston, JSU ,Ohio State University and beyond to respond to critical needs in the face of COVID-19.
